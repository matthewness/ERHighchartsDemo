Area Chart

Area Chart generated from the Model (er.highcharts.demo.model) Company Departments.
Area charts use values in the 
series.data
, so this chart maps the 
data
option in the series to the 
monthlyResults
key path. 
			<wo:ERAreaChart graph="$graph" series="$company.departments" 
			mappings="$mappings" />

Alternative bindings

Alternatively, you can specify the 
seriesNameKeyPath
and 
seriesDataKeyPath
bindings. 
			<wo:ERAreaChart graph="$graph" series="$company.departments" 
			seriesNameKeyPath="name" seriesDataKeyPath="monthlyResults" />

Single Nondefined bindings

You can also just add the bindings that correspond to the HighCharts JS reference directly.
Here, no 'graph' is bound, nor the series name key path, just the 
series
HighCharts JS references. 
			<wo:ERAreaChart series="$company.departments" 
			seriesNameKeyPath="name" seriesDataKeyPath="monthlyResults" 
			plotOptions.area.cursor="pointer" yAxis.title="~'$$$'"
			title.text="Department Financials" exporting.filename="financials_ebitda"
			/>
